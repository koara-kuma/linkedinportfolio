Lesson 1: Champion Abilities

This ability is nunus passive ability. This ability will show an icon above a target when it is ready for use, on use the ability will increase willumps attack speed and movement speed for him and a nearby ally. This will also cause Willumps basic attacks to damage enemies around the target. This passive is what allows willump to have a faster clear time on camps with many targets

This ability is nunus Q ability. This ability can be used to bite either players or monsters/minions. This ability is the main healing for Nunu and is what allows him to have healthy clears. Use this Q inplace of smite to finish off low hp camps and keep a healhty clear. The level 1 damage is 400 and maxes out at 1200. This makes his Q as good as a tier 3 smite at max level.

This is Nunu's W ability. This ability what identifies Nunu as a champion because it is what his ganks are built around. Building speed with this snowball makes the ganks more consistent as your opponents have less time to react to it. All speed buffs activated before the use of W will be held throught the entire duration of Nunu's W. The turn mechanics of snowball is simular to a drift in a car racing game, so learning how to navigate around sharp turns is an essential to mastering this character. Hitting an opponent will result in a high damage output and a CC knockup of the opponent.

This is Nunu's E ability. This ability is a good follow up for Nunu's W. On use this ability with throw snowballs in the direction casted, if the snowballs hit the opponent will be slowed. This ability has 3 casts and after a while the E circle will explode stunning all opponents that have been hit by the snowballs, if they are in the freeze circle.

Lesson three: Rune Page

Dark harvest is essental to Nunu because if Nunu plays his cards right he can farm the souls of low hp players to stack onto his ap damage. This item has no limit to how many souls you can collect meaning Nunu can infintely stack throughout the game. This rune favors mid to late game play and rewards high ganks (which Nunu should do).

The rest of the domniation class act as a way to either further stack, move faster, and do more damage overall.



The final page of runes acts as a way to make Nunu faster by doubling his speed buffs and making him walk faster in the river
